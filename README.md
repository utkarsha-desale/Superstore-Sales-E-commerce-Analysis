# 📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built using the Superstore dataset to analyze sales performance, profitability, and business trends. The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor key business metrics, identify high-performing categories, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to:

- Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
- Identify top-performing product categories.
- Compare sales across different segments.
- Monitor business KPIs through interactive visualizations.
- Provide insights to improve profitability and business performance.

---

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Segment
- Profit Distribution
- Overall Business Performance

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- 📈 Sales Trend Analysis
- 📊 Category-wise Sales Comparison
- 🥧 Sales Distribution by Segment
- 🍩 Profit Distribution
- 📌 KPI Cards for Sales and Profit
- 🎛️ Interactive Filters (Slicers)

---

## 💡 Business Insights

- Identified the categories contributing the highest sales.
- Compared customer segments to understand purchasing patterns.
- Analyzed profit contribution across different categories.
- Enabled interactive filtering for better decision-making.

---

## ✅ Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing categories.
- Improve profitability in lower-performing segments.
- Monitor sales trends regularly to identify growth opportunities.
- Use dashboard insights to support strategic business decisions.
